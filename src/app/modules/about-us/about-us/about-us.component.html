Beauti Skin Clinic is founded by Cinzia Campigotto, professional aesthetician in 2017. Cinzia believes in providing premium but affordable skin treatments and products, as well as creating a warm, clean and friendly environment for every customer.

The clinic specialises in non-surgical and advanced skin treatments for problematic skin such as acne, rosacea, pigmentation and ageing. Our most popular treatments include Aqua 3 Hydrotherapy Facial, Skin Peels, Radio Frequency Skin Tightening, Plasma Face Lift, Micro-Needling with Dermapen, as well as IPL Laser Hair Removal and Skin Imperfection Removal (Electrolysis).

Our waxing is also famous in the area and has received many 5-star online reviews. Thanks to our quick and painless technique, we see happy customers returning all the time.
